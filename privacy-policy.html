Overview

Our Commitment to Privacy

SabyTheNoob ("we", "our", or "us") is an indie game and app studio based in Tamil Nadu, India. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use any of our Android applications published on Google Play.

We take your privacy seriously. Our apps are designed to be fun and accessible — we do not sell your personal data to third parties, and we collect only what is necessary to run the apps and display ads.

By using any of our apps, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

When you use our apps, standard Android and Google Play services may automatically collect technical information such as device type, OS version, and app performance data. This is handled by Google and governed by their Privacy Policy.

Third Parties

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our apps that display ads use Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your Android device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Google Privacy Policy →

Google Play Services

Our apps use Google Play Services for app distribution, updates, and crash reporting. Google Play Services may collect certain device and usage data as described in their own privacy policy.

Google Privacy Policy →

We do not share your personal data with any third party other than the ad networks and platform services listed above.

Children

Children's Privacy

Our apps are rated Everyone / Everyone 3+ on Google Play. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through one of our apps, please contact us at sabythenoob@gmail.com and we will take steps to delete that information.

Your Rights

Your Rights & Choices

  • Right to Know: You can ask us what data we hold about you.
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of any personal data we hold.
  • Opt-out of Ads: Opt out of personalized ads in your Android device settings.
  • Uninstall: You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the app at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of our apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
