About

The Studio

SabyTheNoob is an indie game and app studio built and run by Sabesh Kumar from Tambaram, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.

The studio was born out of a love for games and the drive to create something real — from space simulation in Planet Explorer to escape adventures in The Friction, every app is crafted with intention.

Publishing on Google Play since 2021, growing one game at a time.
