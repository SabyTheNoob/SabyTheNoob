About

The Studio

SabyTheNoob is an indie studio built and run by Sabesh Kumar from Tamil Nadu, India — creating games, mobile applications, VR projects, and virtual simulations.

What started as a passion for game development has grown into a studio that explores interactive experiences across platforms — from space simulation in Planet Explorer to APET applications, train simulations, and immersive VR projects.

Publishing on Google Play since 2021 and constantly expanding. Next up: Office Panic — a meme-style top-down shooter!
